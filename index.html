Drowning Islands

Faraelup Atoll—along with 1500 other low-lying Pacific islands—is disappering.

NASA Sea Level Change

Faraelup is less then 7ft above sea level at its highest point. If the rate of sea rise continues, Faraelup will be under water by 2050.

Re'Metau

or "People of the Open Sea," is a word that describes the people of Faraelup, as well as the people from 700 other islands who share Faraelup's linguistic tradition.
